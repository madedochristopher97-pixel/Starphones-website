Why You Need a Genuine Warranty

Tips & Tricks • April 10, 2026

Don't Get Scammed on Your Next Phone

Buying a smartphone is a significant investment. That's why it is absolutely critical to make sure you are purchasing a genuine device that comes with a legitimate manufacturer's warranty.

The Risks of Grey Market Phones

Phones bought from unauthorized dealers might be slightly cheaper, but they often come with huge risks. They may have been refurbished using substandard parts or have battery issues, and if something goes wrong, you are entirely on your own.

What a Genuine Warranty Gives You

  • Peace of Mind: If a factory defect appears within the warranty period, you get free repairs.
  • Original Parts: Any replacements are guaranteed to be genuine manufacturer parts.
  • Higher Resale Value: A phone with a verified purchase history and warranty is easier to sell later.
